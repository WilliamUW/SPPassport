## 🚶‍♂️ User Flow

1. Connect your wallet 👛🔗
2. Upload your passport photo 📸👆
3. Let our AI wizardry (powered by Gemini API) parse your info 🧠✨
   - We'll extract:
     - First Name 👤
     - Last Name 📛
     - Citizenship Country 🌍
     - Birth Date 🎂
     - Passport Number 🔢
     - Place of Birth 🏙️
     - Date of Expiry ⏳
     - Gender ⚧️
4. Confirm the parsed contents are correct ✅👀

## 🕵️‍♂️ Prove It!
